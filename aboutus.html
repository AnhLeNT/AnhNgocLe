About Us

Welcome To Merida Store

As our slogan is “You can’t explain but you’ll find our gifts that can”, Merida Store is the place you can find unique and sentimental gifts such as shirts, tees, mugs, posters, canvas, blankets… for your loved ones.

We have anything that can be printed here. Our artists and loyal followers are the creative blood in our cottony veins. We strive for bringing only the best and exclusive designs to life.

Let our gifts express your love and even untold feelings towards your family members.

Our Inspiration

We are inspired by finding the need of people who want to give their special ones the most personal, thoughtful presents ever which are not common and can be found in regular stores near you. That’s why we created Merida Store to serve all this.

“Once I happened to view this shop, I found a little white mug with a very funny and special quote that made me laugh non-stop within 5 minutes. I bought it for my mother-in-law’s birthday and she told me that this can be simple but the most memorable gift she ever had. Thank Merida Store a lot or creating such amazing things”

-One of our wonderful feedbacks from customers-
